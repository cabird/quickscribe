Concerns were raised about telemetry access and employer risk (reporting findings could trigger accountability or legal exposure); Chris recommended that any organizational study include suggested mitigations, not just diagnosis. The team agreed Madeline will prototype a short survey combining validated flow/burnout measures and some usage/self-report items, and that Jenna should be consulted regarding telemetry and privacy.
